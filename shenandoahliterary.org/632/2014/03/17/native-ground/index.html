Native Ground

Rob McDonald Click to read more...

Rob McDonald’s photographs have been exhibited nationally and are included several major museum and university collections.  The recipient of a 2013 fellowship from the Virginia Center for the Creative Arts, he has published four books of his photographs, including Birdhouses and, most recently, with Sally Mann and Even Rogers, Cy’s Rollei.  Rob is a professor of English and associate dean of the faculty at Virginia Military Institute.  For more on his work, visit www.robmcdonaldphotography.com.
